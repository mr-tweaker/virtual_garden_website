Bordehi Wildlife
About Us

Preserving wildlife, flora, and fauna, and nature.

Here, animals are taken care of and allowed to live peacefully in their natural habitats. Bordehi wildlife sanctuary in Madhya pradesh is situated approximately 200 Kms South West of Indore District. The Government notified the large geographical extent of Bordehi as wildlife sanctuary on 18th January, 2022 in order to conserve the Indian Elephant. It covers total area of 141 square kilometers of which 25 Km forms the core area of the Sanctuary.

ETHICS

COMMITMENT TO INDEPENDENCE.

Bordehi Wildlife, Inc. (BW-India) is an Indian public wildlife santuary whose mission is to leverage sound science to conserve nature and reduce the most pressing threats to the diversity of life on Earth. We are an independent organization overseen by a Board of Directors with expertise in science, conservation, finance, and public policy, who volunteer their time and service.

All actions, undertakings, and positions of BW-India are determined by the organization alone. BW does not operate as an agent or representative for any funder and does not accept funding from any source that could compromise our independence.
